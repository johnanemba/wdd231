My Country: Kenya

Kenya is my homeland, and I am proud of its rich culture and natural beauty. It is home to the famous and ware the life begin "Big Five" animals and has some of the best game parks in the world. The country is known for its athletic talent, especially in long-distance running, and has earned the title of "Home of the Champions." Kenya’s main income comes from tourism, and the population of approximately 40 million people is made up of 44 different ethnic groups. Our staple foods are Ugali and Chapati, and we are known for our warm hospitality and vibrant culture.
